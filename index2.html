Musings

A brand-new speaker series celebrating the launch of Muse by Robb Report, in partnership with Spring Place. Part Luxury, part thought leadershop, Musings offers a unique live forum in which influential women—trailblazers, tastemakers, idealists, and disruptors—share their views and values on topics ranging from style, design, the arts, travel, wellenss, wealth management, and philanthropy.

About Muse
by Robb Report

Women shape opinion, our culture, our economy and our world. Today's leaders in business, the arts, science and social change are intrepid, innovative and highly original visionaries—women who seek not only to cultivate their own talents and broaden their horizons but also motivate others to undertake their own voyages of discovery. Muse by Robb Report is by and for women of style, substance, and imagination— inspired individuals who inspire us. Meet the modern muse.

About
Spring Place

Spring is where culture, creativity and ideas come together to transform brands. It is an innovative and collaborative marketing ecosystem comprised of a creative agency, a production and events group, and Spring Place, a collaborative workspace and business club. Spring has deep roots in the fashion and luxury sectors and decades of experience working with brands in fashion, beauty, entertainment, music, art and technology.

The global Spring community has headquarters in London, New York and Milan, with future openings set to include Los Angeles and Paris.
